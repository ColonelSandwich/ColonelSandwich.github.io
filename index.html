Hey there! Welcome to My Super Scaler "Pseudo 3D" page, please take a look around!



What's this all about anyways?


This is a site dedicated to my love of the visual technique of "Super Scaler" video games. If you want to know more about the history and a general idea of what it is, click that "What is a Super Scaler" link in the navbar up top!



But why?


Would you believe me if I said "I played Sonic Drift as a kid and thought it was the coolest thing ever so now I research old arcade games for a living"



This is the game that started my fascination. No, really!


Is that all


Well, I was also inspired by Lou Gorenfeld's old page on the same topic, lovingly titled "Lou's Pseudo 3D page", which is still an excellent resource and one I recommend you read as well!
I suppose this site serves as "bookends" of sorts for me, I remember reading this site back around when it was new in 2013 or 2014 and being completely lost. I was new to programming at the time and could hardly understand any of the pseudocode or math that was going on. As the years passed however, I eventually learned the skills necessary to both replicate and understand the effect pretty well.
So I guess in my own little way, this is me saying thanks. I also feel that some of what he goes over could be elaborated a lot more, so I intend to do that as well. Think of it as an extended cut of sorts!



But enough chit-chat, click one of those links up there and start READIN'! Have fun!
